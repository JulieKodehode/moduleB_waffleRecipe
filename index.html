The worlds best waffles

Follow this simple recipe to the worlds best waffles. Crunchy on the outside and soft on the inside. Just as a waffle should be to be perfect.

Ingredients:

Amount: Ingredient:
2 eggs
1dl Sugar
2dl Sour milk
2,5dl Whole milk
350g Flour
1ts Baking powder
½ts Baking soda
1ts Vanilla sugar
½ts Cardamom
125g Melted butter
To make it even better you can add fresh berries and whipped cream as toppings on your waffle serving.

Step by step:

  1. Start by whisking sugar and eggs well together until you get a cream like consistency. Then stir in the milks to the mix until it is nicely blended.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together in a separate bowl, then slowly add the dry mix into the wet mix - while stirring the batter to reduce lumps. And stir until the mix is evenly combined and lump free.
  3. Let the batter swell for 15 minutes. Or even longer if you have time and patience, the more it swells the fuller the waffle will be. Turn on the waffle iron on medium heat so it can prewarm while the batter swells.
  4. Add the melted butter to the mix and stir until combined. (Very important to taste test the batter here)
  5. Add butter or oil on the iron between each waffle to avoid stickiness. And pour a little batter into the iron. Let the waffles turn golden for the best experience of both crunch and soft.
  6. Add butter and jam to your hearts content - and enjoy!
